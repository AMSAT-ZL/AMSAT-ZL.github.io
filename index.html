AMSAT-ZL

The Radio Amateur Satellite Organisation of New Zealand.

Our Objectives are:

1. The promotion and development of space satellites for amateur radio communication and associated experimental, scientific or educational work.

2. The propagation and dissemination of information pertaining to space satellite matters.

3. Support of Amateur Satellite Command stations.

Our Project is KiwiSAT

From home to space, we've built KiwiSAT ready for launch into a Low Earth Orbit.

Kiwi ingenuity, Kiwi know-how, Kiwi spirit.
